Pay-off Balance & Certified Funds

The National Automated Clearing House Association (NACHA) regulations allow the originator of ACH transactions to dispute or return the item to the bank for 60 days. As a result, if you have made any prior payments by telephone check or web check within the last 60 days, your title will be mailed 60 days after the last payment telephone or web check.
