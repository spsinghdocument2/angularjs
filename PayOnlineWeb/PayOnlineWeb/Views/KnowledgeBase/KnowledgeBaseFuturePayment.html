How to Set Future Payment

How to

To create Future MAF Payment select, Set Future Pay option from left side navigation panel

After Clicking Set Future Pay Option you got a pop up window on your screen.

In this screen duplicate payment alert is displaying. If customer already paid their payment for current frequency then schedule any future pay or recurring autopay in same frequency then duplicate pop up alert will be shown.

Please read Popup Instruction carefully & Click button.

After Clicking the button Popup will Disappear & Payment Agreement screen will show on your screen

In this screen customer can see the Payment Agreement and Convenience Fee that will be charged to their account by "CallPass Tech". Customer will select the checkbox of “I agree to the terms and conditions above” for go to the next step.

Please read Payment Agreement carefully & if you Agree With Terms & Condition Click I agree to the terms and conditions & Click button.

After Clicking the button Account Information Screen Will Show.

In this screen customer can see the account details with last payment details.

Please verify your account detail & Click button.

After Clicking the button Amount Payment screen will show here you can set your Future Payment.

To process the Set Future Payment

Step 1. Customer will select the future payment date must between current/coming due date to after next coming due date.
Step 2. Select Payment Method (Pay By ACh or Pay By Debit/Credit Card option).
Case 1. Pay By ACh

If the customer select the radio button of Pay By ACH in select payment method section then ACH type will be shown. These are two types. First one is Saved ACH Detail and second one is New ACH Detail.

Saved ACH Detail

If the customer select the radio button of Saved ACH Detail then all stored ACH details will be shown. Customer can delete any saved ACH details by Click on icon button. Customer can make payment by selecting the radio button of anyone saved ACH detail. If customer will fill all input fields then customer will go to the next step.

New ACH Detail

If the customer select the radio button of New ACH DetailNew ACH Detail then all input fields will be shown. In this screen customer can select the account type like checking and saving by selecting the radio button in account type section. If customer will fill all input fields then customer will go to the next step.

Case 2. Debit/Credit Card

If the customer select the radio button of Pay By Debit/Credit Card in select payment method section then Debit/Credit type will be shown. These are two types. First one is Saved Debit and Credit Card and second one is New Debit and Credit Card.

Saved Debit/Credit Card

If the customer select the radio button of Saved Debit and Credit Card then all stored Debit/Credit details and billing address will be shown. Customer can delete any saved Debit/Credit details by Click on icon button. Customer can payment by selecting the radio button of anyone saved Debit/Credit detail. Customer will input the CVV number in the CVV input field. Customer can edit the billing address of the any saved Debit/Credit Card by Click on edit button in the billing address section.If customer will fill all input fields then customer will go to the next step.

New Debit/Credit Card

If the customer select the radio button of New Debit and Credit Card then all input fields will be shown. In this screen customer can select the card type like Visa and Master by selecting the radio button. If customer will fill all input fields then customer will go to the next step.

After Clicking the button Payment Confirmation Screen Will Show.

Case 1. If the customer select ACH payment Option in before screen.

In this screen customer can see the ACH payment confirmation details with terms and conditions. By Click on button then payment will be done. By Click on button then customer can be edit the payment information’s. By Click on button then payment will be cancel.

In this screen customer can see the Future Pay setup is successfull popup window.

In this screen customer can see the ACH payment confirmation number. By Click on button then customer can print the payment confirmation slip. By Click on button then customer can be edit the payment information’s.

Case 2. If the customer select Debit/Credit Card payment Option in before screen.

In this screen customer can see the Debit/Credit payment confirmation details with terms and conditions. By Clicking on button then payment will be done. By Clicking on button then customer can be edit the payment information’s. By Clicking on button then payment will be cancel.

In this screen customer can see the Debit/Credit payment confirmation pop-up.

In this screen customer can see the Debit/Credit payment confirmation number. By Clicking on button then customer can print the payment confirmation slip.
