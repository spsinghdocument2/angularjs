How to Setup Auto Pay

How to

In this screen customer will register the Autopay. If customer Click on “Click Here To Register” button and “Get Started Now” button then customer will be redirected to Step 1 of autopay.

If customer already done payment and already schedule any payment in current frequency then this duplicate payment alert will be shown. If customer Click on button then customer will be redirect to step 1 of autopay. If customer Click on button then this autopay will be cancelled.

Step 1. Choose Your Payment Method

In this screen customer will select the payment method by Clicking on radio button of Pay By ACH or Pay By Debit/Credit Card. The of “I agree to above terms of use” will compulsory to select by customer after reading this terms and services. If customer Click on button the customer will be redirected to Step 2 of autopay. If customer click on cancel button then this autopay will be cancelled.

Step 2. AutoPay Authorization

In this screen customer can see the Selected Payment Type (ACH or Debit/Credit Card), payment details like amount due, frequency and billing due date.

Case 1. If user select ACH Method in Previous Step

If the customer select Pay By ACH in Step 1. These are two types. First one is Saved ACH Detail and second one is New ACH Detail.

Saved ACH Detail

If the customer select the radio button of Saved ACH Detail then all stored ACH details will be shown. Customer can delete any saved ACH details by Click on icon button. Customer can make payment by selecting the radio button of anyone saved ACH detail. If customer will fill all input fields then customer will go to the next step.

New ACH Detail

If the customer select the radio button of New ACH DetailNew ACH Detail then all input fields will be shown. In this screen customer can select the account type like checking and saving by selecting the radio button in account type section. If customer will fill all input fields then customer will go to the next step.

Case 2. If user select Debit/Credit Card Method in Previous Step

If the customer select the radio button of Pay By Debit/Credit Card in select payment method section then Debit/Credit type will be shown. These are two types. First one is Saved Debit and Credit Card and second one is New Debit and Credit Card.

Saved Debit/Credit Card

If the customer select the radio button of Saved Debit and Credit Card then all stored Debit/Credit details and billing address will be shown. Customer can delete any saved Debit/Credit details by Click on icon button. Customer can payment by selecting the radio button of anyone saved Debit/Credit detail. Customer will input the CVV number in the CVV input field. Customer can edit the billing address of the any saved Debit/Credit Card by Click on button in the billing address section.If customer will fill all input fields then customer will go to the next step.

New Debit/Credit Card

If the customer select the radio button of New Debit and Credit Card then all input fields will be shown. In this screen customer can select the card type like Visa and Master by selecting the radio button. If customer will fill all input fields then customer will go to the next step.

In step 2 all cases customer can see “AutoPay-Recurring Payment Authorization”. It is Compulsory for customer to check the of “terms & Condition” & Click on button the then customer will be redirected to Step 3. And if customer Click button then autopay will be cancelled. And if customer Click on button customer will be redirected to Step 1.

Step 3. Autopay Confirmation

In This Step customer can Review his AutoPay Settings & AutoPay Recurring Payment Confirmation according to selcted payment Type (By ACH or By Dedit/ Credit Card).

Case 1. When User Select ACH Payment Method

In this screen customer Review his Auto Pay Setting for ACH.

In this screen customer can se the payment confirmation details for ACH.

Case 2. When User Select Debit/Credit Card Payment Method

In this screen customer Review his Auto Pay Setting for Debit/Credit Card.

In this screen customer can se the payment confirmation details for Debit/Credit Card.

If customer Click on button then autopay setup confirmation popup will genrate. If customer Click on button popup will hide & customer Click on button and redirect to Setup AutoPay screen. If customer Click on button then autopay will be canceled. If customer Click on button customer will be redirected to step 2.

Setup AutoPay screen

Once user Click on finish button, user redirect to setup AutoPay detail screen.

In this screen customer can se details of allready setup autopay. If customer Click on icon then customer will edit the autopay information.

If customer Click on buton then delete confirmation popup will be shown. if customer Click on button then this autopay will be cancelled. If customer Click on button then this pop-up will be hiden.
