How to Update security Question

How to

In this screen customer can update his security question. This security questions will be helpful for in case of customer forget his password at the time of login. Customer can select the security question accordingly by Click on dropdown and customer can input the answer according to select his questions. If customer Click on button. All security question will be update. If customer Click on button. Customer will be redirect on MAF Account Details screen.
