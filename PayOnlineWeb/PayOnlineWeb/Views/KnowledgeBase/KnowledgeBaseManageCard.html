How to Manage Card

How to

In this Screen customer can see Saved Card Detail & Saved card Billing Address detail. Customer can delete his saved card or Update saved card billing address.

To delete saved card customer can Click on icon for particular card and if user want edit billing address detail Click on button. After Clicking button you can edit your billing address of particular card.

In billing address details two options are available first one is user profile address and second one is last billing address. These address allready stored into the application. If customer Click on button then billing address will be update of selected saved card.
