How to Update Insurance Information

How to

In this screen customer can update the Insurance Infomration like insurance company details, agency/company issuing card details etc. Customer can upload the important document related to the insurance by clicking on button. This document format is JPG, JPEG, PNG only. After fill all insurance information into the input fields. Customer can save all insurance information by clicking on button.
