How to create Account

How to

Online account creation is a 3 step process in which you have to verify the "MAF Account Details" and to provide the "login and security details".

To start the account creation process, go to Login page which is the default page, and Click on Create Account link as shown in image.

Then, you will be redirected to Step 1 for account verification.

Step 1 : Enter Your Account Detail

In this step, enter your "MAF Account Number" and "Account Last Name" from the file/account details. Then Verify the account details by Clicking on button and if information is verifed successfully then you will be moved to Step 2.

Step 2 : Confirm Your Account

In this step, you have to confirm the Account Number and Account Holder Details based on the information from first step. If the account information shown, is correct then tick “Is this the correct Account Holder?” box and Click the button to go to next step otherwise click on button to edit the account information in first step.

Step 3 : Confirm Your Vehicle

This step involves additional verification regarding the vehicle details. The Vehicle details such as Year, Make and Model will be displayed based on the account information provided in first step. If the information displayed is correct then tick “Is this the correct vehicle?” box and Click to finish verification and to setup the account, otherwise Click to edit information in previous steps.

After successful verification of account information, user can create account by providing required details to setup the account as shown in the below image.

User have to fill all the * marked details and to follow the guidelines on the left side, and Click on button to go to next step otherwise Click on button.

Once the entered information will be validated, the account will be created and an email confirmation is send to the customer with login information.

If User want to reset the form or empty the form then Click on button.

User can return to "MAF Login" page anytime from the account creation process, and to do so user have to Click on Return to "MAF Login" page.
